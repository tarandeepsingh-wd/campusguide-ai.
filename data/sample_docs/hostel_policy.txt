Hostel Policy

Room Allocation
Hostel rooms are allocated based on availability, student category, and institute rules. Allocation can change if administrative requirements or maintenance work demand it.

Visitor Rules
Students must follow hostel visitor timing and entry rules. Visitors may need to register at the hostel desk before entering residential areas.

Maintenance Requests
Students can raise maintenance requests for room repairs, electrical issues, plumbing problems, or cleaning concerns. Urgent safety issues should be reported immediately to hostel administration.

Quiet Hours
Residents should avoid loud music, disruptive gatherings, and activities that disturb others during quiet hours.

Disciplinary Action
Violation of hostel rules can lead to warnings, fines, room change, or disciplinary review depending on the severity of the case.
