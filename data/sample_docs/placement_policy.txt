Campus Placement Policy

Eligibility
Students are eligible for campus placement registration if they are enrolled in an approved degree program and have no active disciplinary restriction. A company may define additional eligibility filters such as CGPA, branch, batch, technical electives, or internship availability.

Offer Rules
Once a student accepts an internship or placement offer, the placement office may restrict the student from applying to lower compensation opportunities. Students may be allowed to apply for dream or higher category companies if the placement policy for that season permits it.

Application Deadline
Students must apply before the deadline shown on the placement portal. Late applications are normally not accepted unless the placement office announces an extension.

Interview Conduct
Students must attend all shortlisted rounds unless they have written permission from the placement office. Missing an interview without approval can lead to temporary suspension from campus placement processes.

Document Verification
Students are responsible for keeping their resume, transcript, identity details, and academic records accurate. False information can lead to cancellation of candidature.
