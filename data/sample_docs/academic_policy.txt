Academic Policy

Course Registration
Students must register for courses during the announced registration window. A course may have prerequisites, seat limits, and instructor approval requirements.

Course Drop
Students can drop a course before the official drop deadline. After the deadline, dropping a course is allowed only under exceptional circumstances and requires approval from the academic office.

Attendance
Some courses may define minimum attendance requirements. If a course has an attendance rule, students must satisfy it to remain eligible for evaluation according to the course policy.

Credits and Graduation
Students must complete the required number of credits, mandatory courses, program electives, and institute requirements to graduate. The exact credit requirement is defined by the student's academic program.

Academic Integrity
Cheating, plagiarism, or unauthorized collaboration can lead to penalties such as grade reduction, course failure, or disciplinary action.
